Tele_Chain is a blockchain-inspired compliance framework designed to manage and enforce UCC (Unsolicited Commercial Communication) regulations for telecom and digital communication ecosystems.
It ensures that all commercial messages are consent-driven, traceable, and legally compliant with telecom regulatory norms (e.g., TRAI guidelines).

Unlike traditional anti-spam systems that rely only on content or behavior analysis, Tele_Chain is proactive and regulatory, blocking spam at the source through consent validation, pre-scrubbing, and audit enforcement.
